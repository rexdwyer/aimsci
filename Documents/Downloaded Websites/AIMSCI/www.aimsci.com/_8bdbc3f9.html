Clinical Medicine Books

AIMSCI Cell Med Press publishes both text and reference books in the field of Clinical medicine. Clinical medicine deals with practice of medicine involving patients with regard to diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of disease. AIMSCI Cell Med Press emphasizes evidence-based medicine and the essentialness of randomized controlled trials.

AIMSCI Cell Med Press clinical medicine books are typically solicited from recognized experts in the field. Established professionals are also welcome to submit book proposals for consideration. We are particularly interested in publishing books on such topics as cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, cancer, and skin disorders.

AIMSCI Cell Med Press strongly recommends that clinical medicine books be written based on original literature published in influential peer-reviewed  journals, such as New England Journal of Medicine (NEJM), Lancet, Journal of the American Medical Association (JAMA), British Medical Journal (BMJ), Journal of the American College of Cardiology (JACC), and Circulation, to name a few.
