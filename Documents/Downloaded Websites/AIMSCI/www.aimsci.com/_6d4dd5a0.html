This highly-regarded, evidence-based book by a world-class expert sets the standard for using natural remedies for treating and preventing acne. It is written for those who, or whose loved ones, are inflicted with acne and who want to learn about the most current scientific findings and most definitive clinical evidence behind a wide variety of natural cures of acne. It is a must-read book for all teens and young adults.

Robert Z. Hopkins is Senior Principal Scientist at AIMSCI Research Institute. He is Editor-in-Chief of Reactive Oxygen Species, a peer-reviewed journal for the rapid dissemination of leading edge scientific knowledge on reactive oxygen and related species/entities (including antioxidants) in chemistry, biology, and medicine.
