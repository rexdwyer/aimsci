AIMSCI Cell Med Press Peer-Reviewed Books

For the Reader, not the Author

AIMSCI Cell Med Press books are written in a way that the reader wishes, not the author wants.

We Publish Books in Three Area

AIMSCI Cell Med Press publishes books (primarily textbooks and reference books) in the areas of (1) basic bioscience, (2) clinical medicine, and (3) health promotion.

AIMSCI Cell Med Press emphasizes the importance of cutting-edge medical discoveries reported in highly influential journals and advocates evidence-based medicine. In this context, book writing should be based on findings published in reputable scholarly journals.

Book manuscripts are subject to a rigorous peer-review process sponsored by AIMSCI Cell Med Press and published in both print and e-book versions.

Book proposals are typically solicited from experts in the field. However, experienced professionals are also welcome to directly submit book proposals to AIMSCI Cell Med Press. Please [Click Here] for instruction on book proposal submission.
