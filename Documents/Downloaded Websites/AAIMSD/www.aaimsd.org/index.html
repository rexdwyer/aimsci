Why Does AAIMSD Exist?

AAIMSD is to promote optimal wellness and health.

Mission

The American Association of Innovative Medicine & Standardization (AAIMSD) is headquartered in the vicinity of the great Research Triangle Park (Raleigh, North Carolina), the largest and the most prominent high-tech research park in the USA.

AAIMSD is established to provide solutions for transforming medical innovation and standardization into improved wellness, health, and quality of life for all people throughtout the world.

AAIMSD is guided by four core values: (1) trustworthiness, (2) inclusiveness, (3) transformation, and (4) improvement.
Trustworthiness (by health professionals and general public) is the moral code of AAIMSD.
Inclusiveness (of the Association) is the foundation of AAIMSD.
Transformation (of innovative medicine & standardization) is the purpose of AAIMSD.
Improvement (of the wellness, health, and quality of life) is the ultimate goal of AAIMSD.

Rationale

Although they seem to be contradictory to each other, medical innovation and standardization are in fact closely intertwined. Innovation provides new blood for standardization, and standardization facilitates the transformation of innovation into improvement of wellness, health, and quality of life.

However, there is an ever-increasing lag in timely transforming medical innovation and standardization into improvement of wellness and health for all people throughout the world, especially those in the developing countries (and also the underserved areas in industrialized nations) due to issues such as language barrier, limited accessibility to knowledge base, and lack of continuing training opportunities for both healthcare professionals and patients as well as the general public.

Organizations that address the above lag are in great need, especially in the developing world. AAIMSD aims to providing solutions for minimizing this leg so as to maximize the benefits of medical innovation and standardization throughout the globe, particularly in the developing countries.

What Does AAIMSD Do?

Transforming medical innovation & Standardization

AAIMSD is both a powerhouse and an educational institution.

  • As a Powerhouse
  • As a Training Institution

AAIMSD serves as a powerhouse for the creation of innovative ideas and strategic planning on transforming medical innovation and strandardization into integrative modalities that are readily accessible by all people in need. AAIMSD brings together brilliant talents from diverse fields forming a “think tank”. This in turn fuels the ventures of AAIMSD.

AAIMSD serves as a training institution to assess urgent needs, strategically plan, and implement educational activities for healthcare professionals who seek to provide evidence-based quality care to patients and to the general public who seek to live healthy lives. The educational activities include online training courses, meetings and workshops, and certification. Who Does AAIMSD Serve?

Serving individuals & institutes

AAIMSD serves both healthcare professionals and institutions.

  • Healthcare Professionals
  • Healthcare Institutions

Healthcare professionals [including physicians, nurses, and other practitioners in hospitals, medical centers, clinics, and other facilities] are the chief forces in the healthcare system throughout the world. These professionals determine the outcomes of implementation of medical innovation & standardization. Healthcare professionals also include regulatory and administrative people and policy makers in governmental and non-governmental agencies of healthcare. The decision-making authority of these professionals impacts the adoption of innovative medical/healthcare modalities. AAIMSD serves as a powerful allay for all healthcare professionals in patient care.

Healthcare institutions include medical centers, hospitals, clinics, and other facilities where healthcare practitioners work. In addition, regulatory agencies or organizations of governmental or non-governmental nature, that are dedicated to healthcare, are also considered healthcare institutions. Healthcare institutions working with healthcare professionals make decisions on how to adopt and implement the health-benefiting modalities derived from medical innovations. AAIMSD serves as a powerful ally for all the healthcare institutions in developing institutional policies and procedures to maximize the benefits from medical innovation and standardization.
